About Me

I'm an MBA Candidate at Carnegie Mellon University's Tepper School of Business with a background in Electrical & Computer Engineering. I combine technical depth with strategic product thinking to solve complex, real-world problems in healthcare and beyond.

I'm passionate about building systems that improve operational efficiency and user outcomes. Explore my Professional Work to see these projects in action.
